Q9) Student (rollno, name, marks)

        1) Insert at least 5 records with meaningful data.

        db.student.insertOne({rollno:1,name:"Yash",marks:60})
        db.student.insertOne({rollno:2,name:"Harsh",marks:65})
        db.student.insertOne({rollno:3,name:"Rajveer",marks:70})
        db.student.insertOne({rollno:4,name:"Shreyash",marks:75})
        db.student.insertOne({rollno:5,name:"Avishkar",marks:75})
        db.student.insertOne({rollno:6,name:"Shivani",marks:80})
        db.student.insertOne({rollno:7,name:"Rajlaxmi",marks:85})
        db.student.insertOne({rollno:8,name:"Sahil",marks:85})
        db.student.insertOne({rollno:9,name:"Ram",marks:80})

        2) Display all students.

        db.student.find()

        3) Find the students having marks >70.

        db.student.find({marks:{$gt:70}})

        4) Display students name having marks between 80 and 90.

        db.student.find({marks:{$gt:80,$lt:90}})

        5) Update the marks of “Ram” to 90.

        db.student.updateOne({name:"Ram"},{$set:{marks:90}})

        6) Delete the students having marks = 75.

        db.student.deleteMany({marks:75})

        7) Display top 2 students having highest marks.

        db.student.find().sort({marks:-1}).limit(2)

        8) Increment all student marks by 5%.

        db.student.updateMany({},{$mul:{marks:1.05}})

        9) Display student name having highest marks.

        db.student.find({},{name:1,_id:0}).sort({marks:-1}).limit(1)
