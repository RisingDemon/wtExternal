Student (rollno, name, marks)

        1. Display document of "Ram"

        db.student.find({name:"Ram"})

        2. Marks greater than 80 and less than 90

        db.student.find({marks:{$gt:80,$lt:90}})

        3.	Display name and marks of students having marks equal or greater than 80 and < 90

        db.student.find({marks:{$gte:80,$lt:90}},{name:1,marks:1,_id:0})

        4.	Display name and marks of student having rollno. 1

        db.student.find({rollno:1},{name:1,marks:1,_id:0})

        5.	Display documents in ascending order of name

        db.student.find().sort({name:1})

        6.	Display first 2 documents

        db.student.find().limit(2);

        7.	Display document of Ram or rollno 3

        db.student.find({$or:[{name:"Ram"},{rollno:3}]})

        8.	Display document of Ram and rollno 1

        db.student.find({name:"Ram",rollno:1})

        9.	Display all in documents in descending order of Marks

        db.student.find().sort({marks:-1})

        10.	Display name and marks of student having highest marks

        db.student.find({},{name:1,marks:1,_id:0}).sort({marks:-1}).limit(1)
