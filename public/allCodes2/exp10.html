Q10) Account (accno, branchname, balance)

        1) Insert at least 6 records with meaningful data.

        db.account.insertOne({accno:101,branchname:"Tarabai Park",balance:10000})
        db.account.insertOne({accno:102,branchname:"Nagala Park",balance:15000})
        db.account.insertOne({accno:103,branchname:"Bawda",balance:20000})
        db.account.insertOne({accno:104,branchname:"New Shahupuri",balance:25000})
        db.account.insertOne({accno:105,branchname:"Rajaram Puri",balance:30000})
        db.account.insertOne({accno:105,branchname:"Takala",balance:35000})
        db.account.insertOne({accno:105,branchname:"Shahupuri",balance:33000})

        2) Display all accounts.

        db.account.find()

        3) Find the accounts having balance >25000.

        db.account.find({balance:{$gt:25000}})

        4) Display account numbers having balance between 25000 and 35000.

        db.account.find({balance:{$gt:25000,$lt:35000}})

        5) Update the balance of  accno. 101 to 35000.

        db.account.updateOne({accno:101},{$set:{balance:35000}})

        6) Delete the accounts having balance 15000.

        db.account.deleteMany({balance:15000})

        7) Display top 2 accounts having highest balance.

        db.account.find().sort({balance:-1}).limit(2)

        8) Display total balance for each branch.

        db.account.find({},{branchname:1,balance:1,_id:0})

        9) Display branch name having balance 20000.

        db.account.find({balance:20000},{branchname:1,_id:0})
